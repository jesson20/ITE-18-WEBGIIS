Bong Revilla

Ramon Bautista Bong Revilla Jr. is a Filipino actor, television presenter, and politician serving as a Senator since 2019, and previously from 2004 to 2016. The son of actor-politician Ramon Revilla Sr., he himself became an action star in the 1980s and 1990s as Ramon "Bong" Revilla Jr.
