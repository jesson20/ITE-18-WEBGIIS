About

Mission Statement:

Welcome to The Philippine Mangrove Forest, where we are dedicated to leveraging Geographic Information Systems (GIS) to promote awareness and conservation of mangrove ecosystems. Our mission is to provide a user-friendly platform that allows individuals and organizations to explore, understand preservation of these vital coastal habitats.

History:

Founded in 2023, as an ITE 18 group project, Mangrove Map was born out of a commitment to environmental stewardship. Our journey began with a deep appreciation for the ecological significance of mangroves and a desire to make a positive impact within the ITE 18 program. Over the course of our project, we've evolved into a dynamic platform, connecting enthusiasts, researchers, and conservationists in the common cause of mangrove protection.

Technology Stack:

At the heart of Mangrove Map is a powerful technology stack designed by the ITE 18 - Group 2 to deliver an immersive and informative mapping experience. Our technology stack includes:

Mapping Framework: POSTGIS

Backend Infrastructure: Node.js

Database Management: PostgreSQL

Frontend Development: HTML AND CSS ONLY
