About

Discover the Mangroves of the Philippines

Embark on a virtual expedition to explore the vital mangrove ecosystems of the Philippines. Our website offers an in-depth look at the geographical distribution of mangroves through an interactive map. Delve into the heart of conservation as we highlight key locations, emphasizing the crucial role mangroves play in sustaining our environment. Join us in advocating for the preservation of these unique and indispensable coastal wonders.
